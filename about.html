Mary Kleinschmidt

Hello! My name is Mary Kleinschmidt and I am an artist from Western PA with an interest in graphic design, and a further focus on layout and composition. I love learning methods to arrange compositions so that they are logical but also interesting to look at. I have worked with the magazine at Seton Hill University as a layout editor, as well as The Setonian.
